World Population

This visualization demonstrates the distribution of the world population in 2022 and the correlation between population and other different variables.

World Population Global Overview

This map shows the distribution of the world population on a global scale.

- According to the map, Asia, East Europe, Central America and South America account for a large proportion of population.

World Population Growth Rate and Distribution

This bubble chart explores the correlation between population sum, population growth rate and population density of each country.

- There is no obvious correlation between popuolatino density and population growth rate.

- Countries in Asia, North America and Europe tend to have large population density and medium population growth rate.

- Countries in Africa have small population but high population growth rate.

* Please use the slider to select the minimum population density and use the dropdown menu to select a specific continent you want to explore.

World Population And Average Age Lifespan

This combination of bar chart and line chart shows the sum of population of all countries with the same average age lifespan.

- According to the graph, most of the people live in the countries with an average age of 28 and 38.

* Please use the line chart below to select the specific age group you want to explore by dragging the selection box.

Life Expectancy And Social Support

This scatter plot shows the relationship between healthy life expectancy and social support of each region.

- According to the graph, people from regions with strong social support tend to have longer healthy life expectancy.

- People from Sub-Saharan Africa seem to have the shortest life expectancy in the world.
